Privacy Policy – LoopTok

Last updated: November 2025

This privacy policy describes how the mobile app LoopTok handles user data. LoopTok is developed and published by Nouses.

Data Collection

The app does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details. All video loops and playlists are stored locally on your device.

However, some technical information may be collected automatically by integrated third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: used to display ads in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA/AAID) and anonymous usage data for ad personalization or measurement purposes. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: handles in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment information is managed entirely by Apple and is never visible to the developer.

Permissions Used

  • Photo Library – to allow you to select and loop videos from your photo library.
  • Files – to import and play video files (e.g. MP4) saved on your device.
  • Tracking (AppTrackingTransparency) – used only to request permission for personalized advertising. If declined, only non-personalized ads are shown.

Support Contact

If you need assistance, please contact us at: looptok.help@gmail.com

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any significant changes will be reflected on this page.
